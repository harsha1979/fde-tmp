ATL · Band 7

Associate Forward Deployed Engineer

5+ years experience

Bridge product engineering and strategic customer environments. Translate high-value business requirements into rapid technical implementations. As an Associate FDE, you work directly with customers to deliver outcomes navigating the challenges of a customer environment while bringing product engineering expertise to the customer solution.

Technical Delivery

  • Own end-to-end technical delivery of WSO2 platform deployments API, Identity, Developer, Agent
  • Architect integration solutions connecting enterprise systems, third-party APIs and AI services
  • Mastery in Vibe Coding (AI for Code) and planning AI-native applications (Code for AI)
  • Build custom integrations, connectors and automation pipelines tailored to client workflows
  • Lead platform migrations: on-prem → cloud, application modernisation, agentic enterprise

Customer Engagement

  • Embed in customer teams and traverse the challenges of implementing change
  • Be the trusted advisor for WSO2 technology solutions
  • Translate ambiguous business requirements into concrete technical architectures
  • Run workshops, design sprints and technical deep-dives
  • Identify expansion opportunities by understanding the evolving technical landscape

Required Qualifications

  • 5+ years software engineering experience
  • Hands-on experience with API, integration, identity platforms
  • Proficiency in at least one backend language: Java, Python, Go, or Ballerina
  • Kubernetes and cloud-native deployment (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • IAM/CIAM and application/API security
  • OAuth2, OIDC, REST, GraphQL, event-driven architecture
  • CI/CD pipelines and systems architecture
  • Exceptional communication, customer empathy, resilience

Preferred

  • WSO2 stack or comparable: MuleSoft, Apigee, Kong, Okta
  • LLM/AI integration in enterprise systems
  • Enterprise architecture or solution design background
  • Global customer-facing technical delivery
TL · Band 8

Forward Deployed Engineer

7+ years experience

The FDE works directly with customers to deliver outcomes navigating customer environments, bringing product engineering expertise to the customer solution, and potentially leading the delivery with the customer's team.

Technical Delivery

  • Own and drive end-to-end technical delivery across complex customer environments
  • Design and implement scalable integration architectures connecting enterprise systems, third-party APIs and AI services for production-grade agentic applications
  • Apply strong proficiency in AI-driven development (Vibe Coding) and AI-native application design
  • Build and standardise custom integrations, reusable connectors and automation pipelines
  • Lead platform migrations and modernisation programmes on-prem → cloud, application modernisation, agentic enterprise
  • Design the integration and API infrastructure that powers agentic workflows connecting WSO2 with LLM orchestration frameworks, agents and enterprise data systems

Customer Engagement

  • Embed within customer teams as a trusted technical lead
  • Serve as trusted technical advisor guiding architectural decisions across the WSO2 ecosystem and AI tooling landscape
  • Translate ambiguous business requirements into structured technical designs and implementation plans
  • Lead workshops, architecture design sprints and technical deep-dives with senior customer engineering teams
  • Identify and influence expansion opportunities

Required Qualifications

  • 7+ years software or solution engineering experience
  • Independently own and deliver complex enterprise engagements
  • Production-scale experience with API, integration and identity platforms
  • Proficiency in Java, Python, Go or Ballerina with ability to design production-ready systems
  • Cloud-native/Kubernetes operation (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • IAM, CIAM, API/application security principles
  • OAuth2, OIDC, REST, GraphQL, event-driven architectures
  • System architecture, CI/CD pipelines and modern delivery practices
  • Hands-on integrating LLM/agent services (LangChain, LangGraph, CrewAI) into enterprise systems
  • Strong technical communication for both technical teams and business stakeholders

Preferred

  • Hands-on with WSO2 stack (API Manager, Identity Server, Choreo, Ballerina) or MuleSoft, Apigee, Kong, Okta, ForgeRock
  • Designing agentic enterprise architectures: multi-agent systems, tool-use API patterns, identity-governed agent access
  • Distributed systems and integration patterns
  • Contributions to internal accelerators, open-source projects or technical publications
STL · Band 9

Senior Forward Deployed Engineer

8–12+ years experience

A strategic technical leader extending the FDE role from execution to architecture leadership and transformation ownership. Senior FDEs operate across complex large-scale customer environments bridging product engineering, enterprise architecture and business strategy. You shape and deliver end-to-end transformation outcomes, guiding organisations toward becoming an Agentic Enterprise.

Technical Delivery

  • Architect enterprise-scale integration and agentic solutions multi-system, multi-region delivery programmes
  • Define agentic patterns: tool-use APIs, event-driven agent triggers, identity-governed agent access
  • Drive advanced adoption of AI-native application patterns embedding AI into core enterprise workflows
  • Oversee creation of highly reusable frameworks, accelerators and automation platforms
  • Own end-to-end transformation programmes with accountability for risk, scalability and performance
  • Set the FDE-team standard for Vibe Coding and AI-native design
  • Govern technical quality: architecture reviews, code standards, production-readiness criteria

Customer Engagement

  • Strategic technical partner to senior engineering and architecture stakeholders VP and CTO level
  • Influence customers' technical roadmaps and long-term platform strategy
  • Translate complex business and organisational challenges into future-proof system architectures
  • Lead high-stakes workshops, architecture review boards and executive technical briefings
  • Proactively drive expansion, innovation and platform adoption

Practice Building

  • Maintain engagement playbooks, reference architectures and reusable accelerators
  • Define quality standards for FDE technical deliverables; conduct architecture and code reviews on peer engagements
  • Run internal enablement on advanced WSO2 patterns, agentic architectures and customer engagement
  • Translate patterns from strategic accounts into structured product roadmap input
  • Participate in early-access and beta programmes for new WSO2 capabilities

Required Qualifications

  • 8–12+ years in software engineering, solution architecture or customer-facing engineering
  • Led complex multi-system enterprise integrations and executive technical relationships
  • Deep expertise in API management, IAM/CIAM, enterprise integration patterns and distributed systems
  • Cloud-native, hybrid and multi-region deployments at scale (AWS, Azure, GCP, Kubernetes)
  • Advanced IAM/CIAM, zero-trust architectures, enterprise security models
  • Expert OAuth2, OIDC, SAML, REST, GraphQL, async/event-driven, API ecosystems
  • Systems thinking across security, performance, reliability and cost
  • CI/CD, DevOps and production-grade delivery frameworks
  • Executive communication with CTOs, VP Engineering, architects
  • Full delivery management capability across multi-phase programmes
Architect · Band 10

Principal Forward Deployed Engineer

12–15+ years experience

Top-tier technical authority and strategic advisor operating at the intersection of customer transformation, product evolution and industry direction. Principal FDEs engage with executive stakeholders to define enterprise-wide technology strategies and transformation blueprints, lead the most complex initiatives, and shape how WSO2 enables enterprises globally to realise the agentic enterprise vision.

Technical Vision

  • Define technical and architectural direction for the most complex strategic customer environments globally
  • Establish enterprise-wide and industry-level integration and AI architecture patterns
  • Pioneer next-generation AI-native and agentic architectures
  • Lead the creation of platform-level accelerators and reference ecosystems
  • Sponsor and govern mission-critical transformation programmes

Customer Engagement

  • Operate as a C-level technical advisor CTO, CIO, Chief Architect
  • Shape industry-specific solution strategies and transformation blueprints
  • Translate macro-level business strategy into platform and architecture vision across multiple domains
  • Lead executive engagements: CxO advisory, innovation forums, transformation councils
  • Drive strategic growth, often creating new solution categories and opportunities

Methodology & Practice Leadership

  • Define the WSO2 FDE engagement methodology how engagements are scoped, structured, executed and measured
  • Own the FDE playbook end-to-end as it evolves with WSO2's capabilities and the AI tooling ecosystem
  • Establish WSO2's Agentic Enterprise delivery framework a replicable model from legacy integration to AI-native, agent-driven architecture
  • Conduct architecture reviews on the highest-complexity engagements; set and enforce quality standards
  • Design and deliver advanced technical training for the FDE practice

Product & Strategic Influence

  • Primary technical voice of WSO2's most demanding customers to Product and Engineering leadership
  • Identify strategic gaps between current platform capabilities and agentic enterprise requirements; drive the roadmap
  • Partner with WSO2 Product on Agent Platform strategy: agent-ready API design, identity for AI, event-driven agent triggers, MCP integration

Thought Leadership

  • Represent WSO2 at leading industry conferences on enterprise middleware, API management, identity and agentic AI
  • Publish reference architectures, technical papers and Agentic Enterprise blueprints
  • Build WSO2's presence in the AI-native enterprise architecture community
  • Model and implant a culture of technical excellence and rapid delivery across the FDE team
What we look for

Engineers who can turn ambiguity into shipping software.

Technical depth

Strong APIs, integration, identity and AI agent fundamentals. Code-first. Comfortable in production.

Customer instinct

Empathy, resilience, communications. Comfortable presenting to engineers and to executives in the same week.

Adaptability

Drive results despite shifting requirements. Smaller increments, faster checkpoints, more rework as the ground shifts.

Systems thinking

Architecture across security, performance, reliability and cost not just delivery against a feature list.

Open-source mindset

Build for the customer to run, not for the vendor to keep. Contribute back. Document for extension.

Delivery ownership

Plans, governs risks, manages stakeholder expectations. Outcome over output.
