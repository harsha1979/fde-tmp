Forward Deployed Engineering

Senior engineers, embedded in your team, delivering your agentic outcomes.

Agentic AI is moving faster than fixed-scope SOWs can keep up. WSO2 FDEs sit beside your engineers, write production code, and stay until the business outcome lands not just until a module is deployed.

Outcome-led Embedded Open Standard Agent Fabric ready

Traditional delivery doesn't work in an agentic world.

Models improve every few months. Your thinking on what to automate shifts the moment your first agents ship. New failure modes appear that no runbook covers.

Fixed-scope statements of work, quarterly checkpoints, and clean handovers from vendor to customer all assume a stable target. Agentic AI does not give you one.

The 2-5 year roadmap can no longer hold its ground. You need much more rapid plans, faster execution, and a partner whose incentives are aligned to outcomes, not timesheets.

An engineer on the inside of your problem.

An FDE is a senior WSO2 engineer embedded in your team for the duration of a meaningful business outcome not for a billable task list. They know our platform in depth, they write code, and they are measured on whether your objective lands.

Identify the right use cases

FDEs help you find the handful of agent use cases that genuinely change your cost or speed curve and build those first, before organisational appetite runs out.

Architect & build agentic systems

Tool use, memory, guardrails, evaluation, observability, human-in-the-loop, multi-agent orchestration we bring patterns from other deployments so you don't rediscover every trap.

Build digital resilience in your team

An FDE spends as much time transferring capability as writing code. Your engineers leave the engagement understanding the underlying open source extending it, not filing tickets against it.

Consolidate your stack

A well-designed agentic layer can absorb functionality spread across half a dozen tools. We identify where consolidation is realistic so the programme is self-funding.

Operate with observability

We wire in what you need to govern AI in production including identity-governed agent access and audit trails.

Deliver in weeks, not quarters

Smaller increments, faster checkpoints, more rework as the ground shifts. Built for the realities of agentic deployment not retrofitted from waterfall delivery.

Outcome-led

Open Standard

Everything we help you build sits on open source foundations. An FDE makes you more capable, not more dependent.

Engineering engagements

Deep technical execution from inside your team.

FDEs don't just advise — they own the hardest parts of the work. These are the two high-impact patterns where an embedded senior engineer makes the most difference.

Product Displacement

Replacing a competitor platform with the WSO2 stack through deep technical execution

Customer-side engineering engagement to migrate APIs, auth flows, integrations, and deployments — with full ownership of production outcomes.

Engineering Responsibilities
  • Analyse existing architecture, APIs, auth flows, integrations, and deployment models
  • Compare competitor capabilities with WSO2 technical fit
  • Design migration architecture and integration patterns
  • Build POCs, migration utilities, automation scripts, and reference implementations
  • Solve production blockers with customer engineering teams
  • Optimise performance, scalability, security, and reliability
  • Work directly with WSO2 product and R&D for fixes, roadmap alignment, and enhancements
Engineering Outcomes
  • Successful migration from competitor platform
  • Reduced migration risk and downtime
  • Faster production adoption
  • Reusable migration patterns and deployment blueprints
Replatforming

Modernising legacy systems and moving workloads to scalable, cloud-native architectures

Using WSO2 technologies to build integration layers, CI/CD pipelines, Kubernetes deployments, and AI-ready platform foundations.

Engineering Responsibilities
  • Assess legacy architecture, dependencies, operational bottlenecks, and technical debt
  • Design target-state platform architecture
  • Build integration layers, CI/CD pipelines, Kubernetes deployments, security models, and automation
  • Modernise APIs, identity flows, integrations, and observability
  • Support hybrid, cloud, multi-region, and AI-ready deployments
  • Guide customer engineering teams through implementation and production rollout
  • Troubleshoot performance, scaling, networking, and deployment issues in real environments
Engineering Outcomes
  • Modern cloud-ready platform architecture
  • Better scalability, resilience, and operational efficiency
  • Faster delivery cycles and automation maturity
  • Stable enterprise production deployment with reduced operational overhead
