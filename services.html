Services & Capabilities

The full stack of agentic enterprise delivery owned end-to-end.

FDEs are useful for rapid transformations of operations and business to get desired outcomes. They bring immediate technical expertise, faster Time-To-Value, cost-effective outcome-driven solutions, and a strategic partnership for becoming Agentic.

Where FDEs create value

Four ways an embedded engineer changes the equation.

Making the leap to an agentic AI enterprise

The gains from agentic AI show up when an organisation rewires how work flows not when it bolts a chatbot onto an existing process. FDEs help you identify the handful of use cases where agents will genuinely change your cost or speed curve, and build those first so momentum compounds.

Building digital resilience in your team

We want you to run your own infrastructure. An FDE pairs on PRs, explains architectural trade-offs, and makes sure your engineers understand the underlying open source well enough to extend the system later not just file tickets against it.

Architecting & operating agentic environments

Tool use, memory, guardrails, evaluation, observability, human-in-the-loop, multi-agent orchestration agentic systems introduce complexity traditional integration teams haven't faced. We bring patterns from other deployments so you don't rediscover every trap.

Transforming IT & consolidating vendors

A well-designed agentic layer can absorb functionality spread across half a dozen tools. Part of the FDE's job is to find where consolidation is realistic capturing AI productivity gains and a leaner vendor footprint at the same time. In most engagements, consolidation makes the programme self-funding.
