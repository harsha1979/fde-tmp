Methodology

Built for the parts of agentic delivery that are genuinely uncertain.

The technology is moving. Your own requirements are moving. The partner you want is one whose incentives, working model, and technical foundations are built for that reality not retrofitted to it.

Where we sit

Between consulting, product engineering and customer success.

Most engagements in the market sit on one side or the other. Consultancies plan, advise and provide expertise. Support teams react to tickets. Customer success teams run QBRs.

An FDE is none of these. It is an engineer on the inside of your problem, paid to solve it with you in full from figuring out what needs to be solved, to how it should be designed, implemented and deployed to deliver the outcome you need.

How an engagement works

From scoping to outcome in weeks, not quarters.

1

Scope the outcome

We work with your team to shape a first objective and agree how to measure it. Not features, not tasks a measurable business outcome.

2

Embed the FDE

A senior WSO2 engineer joins your standups and planning. They sit alongside your engineers, take ownership, and start shipping.

3

Iterate fast

Smaller increments, faster checkpoints, continuous re-scoping as priorities shift. We adjust architecture, models and workflow as we learn.

4

Transfer capability

Pair on PRs, run workshops, document architecture. Your team leaves the engagement able to extend, run and operate the system independently.

WSO2 Agent Fabric

Pick what you need. Scale as you grow.

The fabric is built with our mature, seasoned product stack and it blends well with agentic enterprise implementation. Five platforms, working as one.

Four principles

Why our incentives are built for moving ground.

1

Outcome-led

Engagements organised around measurable OKRs up front, then iterate on whatever mix of platform, integration, model and workflow gets you there. Cross-functional pods reorganise around customer OKRs and rewrite scope as priorities change. Smaller increments, faster checkpoints, more rework as the ground shifts.

2

Embedded

FDEs work inside your team. Standups, planning, ownership. The gap between a vendor who understands their platform and a partner who understands your business is where most agentic projects stall. Embedding closes it. No knowledge-transfer ceremony at the end of the day.

3

WSO2 Agent Fabric

A mature, seasoned product stack: Agent, API, Identity, Integration and Engineering platforms. Every layer of governance for the agentic enterprise is already there secure, scalable, observable, with identity-governed access for both humans and agents.

4

Open source

Everything we help you build sits on open source foundations. Not a stylistic preference a resilience principle. Read the code, fork it, keep running it if our commercial relationship ends tomorrow. That's what "getting the best of open source" looks like in practice.

Derisk an agentic programme without slowing it down.

Outcome-led, so we are paid to care about the same things you care about. Embedded, so we feel what you feel. Open source at its core, so the capability you build with us stays with you.
